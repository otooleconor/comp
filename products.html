Greenhouse Soil Moisture Detector(GMD)

,

This artefact solves a very specific problem for users who own greenhouses or conservatories. It automatically sensor the moisture in the soil of the greenhouse and if it detects it has low moisture or is "too hot" then it will pump water into the soil in the greenhouse.
