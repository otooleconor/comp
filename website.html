What we do:

At Infinity we design and implement many smart home systems. Our staple product and most popular is our mechanical greenhouse arrangement. This product detects when a greenhouse, conservatory or anywhere a user stores plants or vegtables gets too hot and cools down the room by pumping water stright into the room.

Extra information on smart home systems

The Mechanical greenhouse arrangement uses multiple things to work

  • 2 microbits
  • Moisture Sensor
  • Relay
  • Light system
  • IoT bit
  • Water pump
